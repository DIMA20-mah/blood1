Frequently Ask Questions

Anyone aged 18-65, in good health, and weighing at least 50kg can donate blood. Please consult with our medical staff if you have specific health concerns.

The donation process takes about 30-45 minutes, including registration, donation, and recovery time.

Most donors feel only a slight pinch at the beginning. The donation process is generally painless.

Drink plenty of water, eat a healthy meal, and get a good night's sleep before donating blood.

You can donate whole blood every 8 weeks. Platelet donors can donate every 2 weeks.
